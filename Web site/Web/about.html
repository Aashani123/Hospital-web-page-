ABOUT RAR HOSPITAL


History of Our Hospital


RAR Hospitals Corporation PLC commenced operations in Sri Lanka on July 7, 1996 under the brand name Appel Hospitals, part of the Apel Hospitals chain established by Dr. Pradeep Reddy, a renowned physician in India. In 2015, we celebrated a great decade of healthcare. Over the past decade, RAR Hospitals has revolutionized the healthcare sector in Sri Lanka through the development of infrastructure and the advancement of its products and services through significant investments, with the aim of providing health care on par with global developments in medical technology.


Facilities and Services of Our Hospital


Our Hospitals is a multi-specialty tertiary care hospital with 500 beds spread over 400,000 square feet and 4 acres of beautiful gardens. It offers state-of-the-art features complemented by state-of-the-art technology and is staffed by a well-experienced and trained team. We provide the full range of the latest diagnostic and advanced medical technology. Our 11-story structure is equipped with a helicopter and is the only private medical facility in Sri Lanka equipped with air ambulance services.
